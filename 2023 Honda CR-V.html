2019 Honda CR-V

The 2019 Honda CR-V offers a smooth, responsive driving experience with a turbocharged engine and exceptional fuel economy. A popular choice for families, it features a spacious cabin with modern technology and safety features. Whether for daily commutes or long road trips, the CR-V combines comfort and practicality.

Turbocharged Engine

Enjoy enhanced performance with a fuel-efficient 1.5L turbocharged engine.

Spacious Interior

The CR-V provides ample room for passengers and cargo with high-quality materials and a modern design.

Advanced Safety Features

With Honda Sensing suite, the CR-V comes equipped with adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and more.

With up to 28 mpg city/34 mpg highway, the CR-V makes fuel savings a priority.
