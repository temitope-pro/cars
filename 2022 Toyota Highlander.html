2022 Toyota Highlander

The Toyota Highlander is a mid-size SUV that combines comfort, reliability, and advanced technology. Whether you're navigating urban streets or venturing into the great outdoors, the Highlander offers the perfect balance of space and performance.

Performance

Powered by a robust V6 engine, the Highlander offers a smooth and responsive driving experience.

Design

With its modern design and spacious interior, the Highlander provides an elevated driving experience for the whole family.

Technology

Equipped with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a premium audio system, the Highlander keeps you connected on the go.

Safety

The Toyota Safety Sense suite includes lane-departure warning, adaptive cruise control, and pre-collision systems to ensure your safety.

Explore the 2022 Toyota Highlander

The 2022 Toyota Highlander is a top choice for families who value safety, comfort, and versatility. From its spacious cabin to its advanced tech features, the Highlander is designed to enhance every journey.
