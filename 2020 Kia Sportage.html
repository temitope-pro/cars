Embrace the Road with Style and Power

The 2019 Kia Sportage

The 2019 Kia Sportage seamlessly blends rugged adventure with refined sophistication. It boasts a bold design, a refined interior, and cutting-edge technology, making it a perfect companion for both city and off-road driving. With its turbocharged engine and top-tier safety features, the Sportage ensures you're ready for any journey.

Key Features

Turbocharged Engine

The 2.0L turbocharged engine offers 240 horsepower for impressive acceleration and smooth handling.

All-Wheel Drive

Equipped with an available all-wheel-drive system, the Sportage is ready for any weather or terrain.

Advanced Safety Features

Drive with confidence thanks to features like lane-keeping assist, forward collision warning, and rear cross-traffic alert.

UVO Infotainment System

Stay connected with the UVO infotainment system, supporting Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and available navigation.

Drive the Change

Discover the perfect balance of performance, technology, and comfort with the 2019 Kia Sportage. Whether navigating city streets or embarking on an adventure, the Sportage will help you make the most of every drive.
